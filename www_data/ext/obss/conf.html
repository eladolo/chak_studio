Requirements

- You need to install the OBS websocket plugin.
- You need to use ngrok.com to create a public url for your localhost.
- Once you login in to ngrok use the following instructions to make run ngrok on your computer, and use the following command to create your url:
ngrok http {obs port}
ngrok http 4444

Usage

- Save your credentials on this panel.
- On your dashboard page in the panel of extensions you will have:
  . a button to start/stop streaming OBS.
  . a button by each scene you have on your OBS.
  . a button to close de websocket connection to OBS.
- When stream start, on your dashboard/channel video you will have an overlay with :
  . a button to stop streaming OBS.
  . a button by each scene you have on your OBS.
  . a button to close de websocket connection to OBS.
       * If the extension can't connect to OBS, you will have a login form to fix the problem.
