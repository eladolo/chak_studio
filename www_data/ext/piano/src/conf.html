Requirements

- Bits actives in your channel if you want to active bits for viewers

Usage

- Save configuration on this panel.
- Trigger the piano from the dashboard or from the overlay, and/or let your viewers trigger the piano if you choose to do so.
. If your choose to let trigger piano for bits, if the bits process completed with success the notes will be send to all the viewers in channel and chat.
